# Tides and the Earth-Moon Barycenter

## Concept

Show a simplified liquid-covered Earth being stretched into an elliptical water envelope along the Earth-Moon line.

## Key Explanation

- Earth and Moon orbit a shared center of gravity called the barycenter.
- A perfectly liquid-covered Earth would not remain exactly spherical.
- The Moon's gravity and the Earth-Moon orbital motion stretch the liquid envelope along the long axis.
- The tide shape is exaggerated in the animation so the mechanism is visible.
