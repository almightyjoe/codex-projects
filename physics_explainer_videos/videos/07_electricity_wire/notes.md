# How Electricity Actually Moves Through a Wire

## Files

- `electricity_in_wire.mp4`: rendered video
- `preview.png`: still preview frame

## Concept

- The battery sets up an electric field in the circuit.
- Positive copper ions remain fixed in the metal lattice.
- Mobile electrons drift through the lattice from one site to the next in the simplified view.
- Electron flow and conventional current are shown in opposite directions.
- In the tungsten filament, resistance and collisions turn electrical energy into heat and light.
